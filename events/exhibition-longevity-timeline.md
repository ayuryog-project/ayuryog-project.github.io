LONGEVITY: A Timeline of Yoga and Ayurveda

 

*An exhibition curated by Jacqueline Hargreaves for AYURYOG*

 

**PLEASE NOTE: Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the opening of the exhibition is postponed until further notice.**

 

 

Were health and well-being always a goal of practitioners of yoga? Did ayurvedic physicians use yoga to achieve health and liberation for themselves or their patients? This timeline highlights some of the key milestones and significant evidence for the entangled histories of yoga and Ayurveda. It leverages from the most recent research discoveries in the field of ayurvedic and yoga studies as identified by the AYURYOG project, University of Vienna.
