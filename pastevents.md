## 2017

### Presentation by Suzanne Newcombe: 'A Historical Overview on Yoga as a Medical Intervention'
**Nehru Centre, London — 23 June 2017**

The Nehru Centre in association with The All Party Parliamentary Group on Indian Traditional Sciences organised a one-day Symposium to mark the 3rd UN International Day of Yoga. Dr Suzanne Newcombe gave a talk at 10.50am on 'A Historical Overview of Yoga as a Medical Intervention'.

---

### Panel discussion at the Vienna International Centre for International Yoga Day
**Vienna International Centre — 21 June 2017**

AyurYog team member Suzanne Newcombe was invited to participate in a panel discussion on 'Yoga, Wellness and Nutrition' to mark the International Day of Yoga. The event was organised by the Permanent Mission of India to the United Nations.

### Philosophy in Contemporary Yoga Symposium — Triyoga, London
**Triyoga, London — 2017**

A teachers' symposium seeking to identify philosophy's place in the teaching of modern yoga, and to help yoga teachers better understand how they can translate ancient — sometimes esoteric — concepts and ideas into action in everyday life on and off the mat.

Panelists: Hamish Hendry, James Mallinson, Suzanne Newcombe, and Carlos Pomeda.

### AyurYog Workshop: Rejuvenation, longevity, immortality
**University of Vienna — 21–22 October 2016**

Perspectives on rasāyana, kāyakalpa and bcud len practices.

The two-day workshop explored the historically linked South and Inner Asian practices of *rasāyana*, *kāyakalpa* and *bcud len* — practices, treatment techniques and formulations aimed at goals ranging from prolonging life, restoring youthfulness, and promoting physical health, to accessing special powers, achieving enlightenment, and attaining immortality. See the [blog post]({{ '/blog/workshop-rasayana-kayakalpa-bcud-len/' | relative_url }}) for further details.
