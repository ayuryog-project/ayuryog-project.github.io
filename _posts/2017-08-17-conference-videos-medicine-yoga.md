From August 1-3, our conference "[Medicine and Yoga in South and Inner Asia](https://web.archive.org/web/20220812151146/http://ayuryog.org/event/conference-medicine-and-yoga-south-and-inner-asia-body-cultivation-therapeutic-intervention)" took place at Vienna university. Twenty-seven speakers and over seventy participants from all over Europe discussed aspects of South and Inner Asian medicine. The focus of the AyurYog project and its invited speakers was on longevity practices as a shared concern in South Asian medical, alchemical and yogic milieus and both presentations and discussions were lively!** **

Videos of several of the conference presentations can now be viewed on the[ AyurYog YouTube channel](https://web.archive.org/web/20220812151146/https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC4ssviEb_KoAtb2U_XaXf_w). Also look out for the blogpost by [The Luminescent](https://web.archive.org/web/20220812151146/https://theluminescent.blogspot.ca/2017/08/medicine-and-yoga-in-south-and-inner.html) which features the conference videos together with abstracts and pictures of the conference.
