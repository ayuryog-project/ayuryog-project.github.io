Following my [online interview with Jacqueline Hargreaves]({{ '/resources/untangling-traditions/' | relative_url }}), many of you asked where you could find the text of the *Dharmaputrikā*.

The *Dharmaputrikā*, the "Little Daughter of Dharma", is an early yoga manual that includes elaborate descriptions of methods for overcoming obstacles to success in yoga, as well as methods for curing diseases. Chapter Ten is dedicated to *yogacikitsā* — medical treatment in the context of yoga practice — and brings together āyurvedic therapeutic categories with yoga's distinctive understanding of the body and its subtle physiology.

This post provides an introduction to the chapter and some preliminary observations on its medical content.
