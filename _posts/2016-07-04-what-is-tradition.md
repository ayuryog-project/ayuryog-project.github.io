The popular "Yoga Poster" depicts the history of yoga as a branching tree with its roots in the "Vedic Period" and leaves encompassing all forms of yoga practised today. This image is appealing, but it raises significant historical questions.

The tree metaphor implies a single origin, continuous growth, and clear lines of descent. The historical reality is messier: multiple origins, discontinuities, cross-pollinations, and reinventions. The AyurYog project's emphasis on "entangled histories" is partly a response to this — an attempt to capture the genuinely complex relationships between yoga, ayurveda, and alchemy without flattening them into a single narrative of tradition and transmission.
