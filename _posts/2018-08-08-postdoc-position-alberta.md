We are currently seeking a post-doctoral researcher to join the AyurYog project at the Department of History and Classics at the University of Alberta in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada.

The position is focused on the history of South Asian alchemy (*rasaśāstra*) and will involve close collaboration with the project's Principal Investigator and the broader team. Candidates should have expertise in Sanskrit, Indian intellectual history, and ideally some background in the history of science or medicine. The position offers an opportunity to work on a significant collaborative project at the intersection of philology, history of science, and digital humanities.

For full details, please contact the Department of History and Classics at the University of Alberta.
