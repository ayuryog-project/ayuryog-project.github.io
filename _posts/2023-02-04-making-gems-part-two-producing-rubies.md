The fish black is ready! This is the base product needed for producing a whole series of gems: rubies, sapphires, emeralds, garnet, topaz, and blue sapphires.

Time to start making those rubies!

The recipe from the *Rasaratnākara* (*Vādakhaṇḍa*, chapter 19) is characteristic of Sanskrit alchemical literature: precise about ingredients and quantities, but sparing in its description of procedure. Working out what the text means in practice — which is the whole point of the reconstruction series — requires combining close philological reading with hands-on experimentation.
