ERCcOMICS is a project that uses visual storytelling to communicate the contents of European Research Council projects to a broad public. This year, AyurYog was selected as one of the projects to be represented as a cartoon. The artist, Alice Milani, spoke with Principal Investigator Dagmar Wujastyk and then produced a short comic strip introducing the project's themes: the entangled histories of yoga, ayurveda, and Indian alchemy.

The comic is a reminder that the history of these traditions is more complex — and more interesting — than most popular accounts allow.
