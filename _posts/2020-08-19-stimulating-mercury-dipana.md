We have arrived at the eighth, and for us final, procedure in the *Rasahṛdayatantra* series: the stimulating or kindling (*dīpana*) of mercury.

The *Rasahṛdayatantra* (chapter 2, verse 11) describes this step as reinvigorating the mercury with a combination of herbal and mineral substances, so that it is fit to be used in the production of elixirs. With this step, the eighteen-procedure treatment of mercury is substantially complete — though we have reconstructed only the first eight in this series, covering the foundational operations of the text.
