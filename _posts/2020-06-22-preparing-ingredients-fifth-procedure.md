Before undertaking the fifth procedure (*pātana*) in the *Rasahṛdayatantra* series, several preliminary preparations are required: making copper powder, purifying copper powder, and purifying copper sheet. Each of these is a miniature exercise in interpretive reconstruction — the text gives the outcome, but the procedure must be worked out from a combination of the primary source, later commentaries, and practical experiment.
