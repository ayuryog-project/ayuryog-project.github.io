The joint conference of AyurYog with three other Vienna projects on Asian medicine is taking shape, and we are delighted to announce the programme. The conference — "Medicine and Yoga in South and Inner Asia: Body Cultivation, Therapeutic Intervention and the Sowa Rigpa Industry" — will take place August 1–3 at Vienna University, and is open to the public.

Twenty-seven speakers from across Europe and beyond will discuss aspects of South and Inner Asian medicine, spanning classical Sanskrit, Tibetan and Pali sources through to contemporary ethnographic and sociological perspectives. Topics include āyurvedic pharmacology, yoga physiology, the history of rasaśāstra, and the globalisation of traditional medicine.
