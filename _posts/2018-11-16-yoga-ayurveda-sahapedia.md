Suzanne Newcombe has just published a short encyclopedia-style article on the relationship between [Yoga and Āyurveda](https://www.sahapedia.org/yoga-and-%C4%81yurveda#_ednref25) as understood to date, which might be a helpful point of orientation for those coming to this subject for the first time. 

[Sahapedia](https://www.sahapedia.org) is an open online resource on the arts, cultures and heritage of India. “Saha”, Sanskrit for “together with”, is an invitation to explore together the richness of our cultural landscapes.

This article is part of a [series of Sahapedia articles](https://www.sahapedia.org/hatha-yoga) emerging in collaboration with the Haṭha Yoga Project (HYP).
