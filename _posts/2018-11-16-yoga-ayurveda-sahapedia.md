Suzanne Newcombe has published a short encyclopaedia-style article on the relationship between [Yoga and Āyurveda](https://www.sahapedia.org/yoga-and-%C4%81yurveda) on Sahapedia, a collaborative digital platform for the arts, cultures and heritage of India.

The article provides an accessible introduction to how the two disciplines have intersected historically, and is intended as a point of orientation for those coming to this subject for the first time. It covers the shared vocabulary of the two traditions, key historical moments of convergence, and the contemporary context in which both yoga and ayurveda are increasingly practised globally.
