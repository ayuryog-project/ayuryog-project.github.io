From August 1–3, our conference "Medicine and Yoga in South and Inner Asia" took place at Vienna University. Twenty-seven speakers and over seventy participants from all over Europe discussed aspects of South and Inner Asian medicine. The conference was jointly organized with three other Vienna-based projects on Asian medicine.

For the full programme see the [conference page]({{ '/events/conference-medicine-yoga-2017/' | relative_url }}).
